Landmark

Before starting to design visualization, we asked some friends in Shanghai and their elders, what are the real questions they talked about in each period, and the corresponding answers. When they talked about landmarks in Shanghai, the answers were varied. For the most famous landmark, Shanghai residents of different age groups have different answers at different times. We collected their answers and used mapbox to create this storytelling interactive map.
